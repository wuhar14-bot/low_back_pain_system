Step 1: Red Flag Screening
(e.g., neurological deficits, cancer history, trauma)
↓
Exit Point: Positive red flag → Immediate referral to specialist
↓
Step 2: Yellow Flag Assessment
(e.g., psychosocial risks like fear-avoidance, depression)
↓
Step 3: History and Physical Examination
(e.g., mechanical pain characteristics, normal neuro exam)
↓
Exit Point: Abnormal findings → Imaging and specialist referral
↓
Step 4: Imaging (if needed)
(e.g., only for red flags or failed conservative treatment)
↓
Exit Point: Structural pathology found → Exclude from NSLBP pathway
↓
Final Diagnosis: Non-Specific Low Back Pain (NSLBP)
Eligible for study recruitment and AlignPro app download
